My name's Andrea Picciau (it's pronounced _pee-cha-hu_, with the stress on the
_a_). I work on software for scientific computing and parallel computer
architectures such as GPUs, multi-core CPUs, and computer clusters. In
particular, I am interested in linear algebra and optimization algorithms.

Currently, I am a senior software engineer in test at [MathWorks
UK](https://www.mathworks.com), in the Parallel Computing Toolbox team. I am
responsible for the quality of MATLAB's GPU software for numerical algorithms
and deep learning.

Before working at MathWorks, I did a PhD in Electronic Engineering at [Imperial
College London](https://www.imperial.ac.uk), where I worked on low-level
optimizations of sparse linear algebra algorithms for GPUs.

You can find my complete CV on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/apicciau).
